Introduction

BeepBoop+ Period ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our period tracking application.

Important: We understand the deeply personal and sensitive nature of menstrual health data. Your privacy and security are our absolute top priorities. We have designed this app with privacy-first principles to ensure your intimate health information remains under your complete control.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (AsyncStorage). We do not have access to this data unless you explicitly choose to sync it:

  • Period cycle data (start dates, end dates, flow intensity)
  • Symptom tracking (cramps, mood, energy levels, etc.)
  • Medication logs (birth control, pain relief, supplements)
  • Journal entries (daily notes, mood tracking, intimate thoughts)
  • Cycle predictions and fertility window calculations
  • Personal preferences (themes, settings, notification preferences)
  • Premium subscription status

Optional Cloud Data (Only If You Choose to Connect)

If you choose to use our supporter connection features (to link with partners, family, or friends), the following data may be synced to the cloud:

  • User ID (randomly generated, not linked to personal identity)
  • Connection codes and relationship data (supporter/tracker linkages)
  • Period countdown information (only what you explicitly permit)
  • Support requests and messages
  • Premium subscription status (for validation purposes)

You control what is shared. See "Your Privacy Controls" below.

Automatically Collected Data

  • Device Information: We do not collect device identifiers or analytics
  • Usage Data: We do not track your app usage
  • Crash Reports: We do not collect crash analytics (Expo default telemetry is disabled)

Data Storage & Security

Local Storage

  • All sensitive data is stored on your device using AsyncStorage
  • Data is encrypted at the device level (standard mobile OS encryption)
  • You can delete all data at any time

Cloud Storage (Optional)

  • Cloud database is used only for supporter connection features
  • Data is secured with authentication rules
  • We do not store detailed cycle or symptom data in the cloud
  • Only countdown and connection metadata is synced
  • Data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS)

Payment Processing

  • We do not collect or store credit card information

Data Retention & Deletion

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data - Export your data via CSV at any time
  • Delete Your Data - Delete all data from the app at any time
  • Modify Your Data - Edit or delete individual entries anytime
  • Revoke Permissions - Change what supporters can see anytime
  • Request Account Deletion - Contact us to delete Firebase connection data

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Retained until you delete the app or clear data
  • Firebase Data: Retained while you have active connections
  • Messages: Retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted
  • Connection Codes: Expire after 7 days if unused
  • Subscription Data: Retained as required by law (typically 7 years for financial records)
