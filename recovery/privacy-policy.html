Introduction

BeepBoop+ Recovery ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our addiction recovery support application.

Important: We understand the sensitive nature of recovery data. Your privacy and anonymity are our top priorities. We have designed this app with privacy-first principles.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (AsyncStorage). We do not have access to this data unless you explicitly choose to sync it:

  • Substance tracking data (substances tracked, quit dates, streak information)
  • Check-in records and daily logs
  • Journal entries (mood, gratitude, thoughts, triggers)
  • Relapse logs and reflections
  • Money saved calculations
  • Goals and coping tool usage
  • Personal preferences (themes, settings, grace period settings)
  • PIN lock code (if enabled)
  • Premium subscription status

Optional Cloud Data (Only If You Choose to Connect)

If you choose to use our connection features (to link with supporters), the following data may be synced to the cloud:

  • User ID (randomly generated, not linked to personal identity)
  • Connection codes and relationship data (supporter/recovery user linkages)
  • Messages between users
  • Shared streak data (only what you explicitly permit via granular permissions)
  • Premium subscription status (for validation purposes)

You control what is shared. See "Your Privacy Controls" below.

Automatically Collected Data

  • Device Information: We do not collect device identifiers or analytics
  • Usage Data: We do not track your app usage
  • Crash Reports: We do not collect crash analytics (Expo default telemetry is disabled)

‚õî DATA WE DO NOT COLLECT ‚õî

  • We do NOT collect your name, email, phone number, or any personally identifiable information
  • We do NOT collect your location data
  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties
  • We do NOT use tracking pixels or advertising identifiers
  • We do NOT share your recovery data with anyone without your explicit permission

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  1. Provide core app functionality (streak tracking, journaling, connections)
  2. Sync data between your devices (only if you enable cloud sync)
  3. Enable supporter connections (only if you create connection codes)
  4. Process premium subscriptions (via Google Play)
  5. Send push notifications (only if you enable them)

We do NOT use your data for:

  • Advertising targeting
  • Analytics or data mining
  • Selling to third parties
  • Any purpose other than app functionality

Data Storage & Security

Local Storage

  • All sensitive data is stored on your device using AsyncStorage
  • Data is encrypted at the device level (standard mobile OS encryption)
  • PIN lock adds an additional layer of security
  • Emergency nuke button allows instant data deletion

Cloud Storage (Optional)

  • Cloud database is used only for connection features
  • Data is secured with authentication rules
  • We do not store sensitive recovery details in the cloud
  • Only connection metadata and permitted streak data is synced
  • Data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS)

Payment Processing

  • Payments are processed through Google Play Store
  • We do not collect or store credit card information
  • Google Play handles all payment data securely
  • We only receive a subscription status confirmation

Data Retention & Deletion

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  1. Access Your Data - Export your data via CSV at any time
  2. Delete Your Data - Use the Emergency Nuke button to delete everything
  3. Modify Your Data - Edit or delete individual entries anytime
  4. Revoke Permissions - Change what supporters can see anytime
  5. Request Account Deletion - Contact us to delete Firebase connection data

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Retained until you delete the app or use the nuke button
  • Firebase Data: Retained while you have active connections
  • Messages: Retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted
  • Connection Codes: Expire after 7 days if unused
  • Subscription Data: Retained as required by law (typically 7 years for financial records)
